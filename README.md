# 🔍 Tracer  
### *Tracing truth in every claim.*

---

## 🚀 Overview

**Tracer** is a **real-time, explainable, two-sided risk intelligence platform** designed to bring **trust and transparency** into fraud-prone decision systems such as insurance, healthcare, banking, and reimbursements.

Instead of making rigid **fraud / not-fraud** decisions, Tracer **quantifies risk on a continuous scale (1–100)** and clearly explains *why* a case was flagged — enabling **better decisions, fairness, and human oversight**.

---

## ❓ Why Tracer?

Most fraud detection systems today ask only one question:

> *“Is the customer fraudulent?”*

We believe that question is incomplete.

**Trust is two-sided.**

- Companies need to assess **customer risk**
- Customers deserve insight into **company reliability**

**Tracer is built to do both.**

---

## 🌟 Key Innovations (What Makes This Unique)

### 🧠 1. AI-Generated, Privacy-Preserving Dataset
- No sensitive real-world data used
- Behaviorally realistic synthetic data
- Proper class imbalance and noise
- Ethical, safe, and production-aligned

---

### 🌍 2. Domain-Agnostic Design
- Not hardcoded to insurance
- Applicable across:
  - Insurance
  - Healthcare claims
  - Banking transactions
  - Expense reimbursements
- **Same engine, different domain — no retraining required**

---

### 🔁 3. Two-Sided Intelligence (Rare & Powerful)

| Model | Purpose |
|------|--------|
| **Customer Risk Model** | Helps companies assess claim risk |
| **Company Trust Model** | Helps customers evaluate insurer reliability |

> Similar to Amazon: buyers rate sellers, and sellers evaluate buyers.

---

### ⚡ 4. Real-Time Risk Scoring (1–100)
- No binary fraud labels
- Continuous risk percentage
- Preserves uncertainty
- Supports prioritization instead of accusations
- Enables configurable thresholds and human review

> **Binary models decide.  
Risk models inform.**

---

### 🔍 5. Explainable AI (XAI)
- Every risk score includes clear explanations
- No black-box decisions
- Transparent, auditable, regulator-friendly

---

### 💬 6. Grounded AI Chatbot
- Knowledge-base driven (no hallucinations)
- Explains:
  - Risk scores
  - Claim reviews
  - Document requirements
  - Processing timelines
- Explicitly says *“I don’t know”* when information is missing
- Escalates to human support when required
